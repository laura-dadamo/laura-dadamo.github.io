Clinical Experience & Training

My clinical training has spanned individual and group psychotherapy, consultation, and assessment with patients across the lifespan, with a focus on anxiety, eating disorders and body image, mood (e.g., depression), and the intersection of mental and physical health (e.g., health behavior change, coping with chronic illness). I have trained across a range of settings including a university clinic, group practices, and medical centers at outpatient and inpatient levels.
