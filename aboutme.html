About Me

I am a PhD Candidate in Clinical Psychology at Drexel University. My research examines what maintains disordered eating in everyday life and develops scalable, technology-enabled interventions to address those factors. I also study how to get these digital supports into settings where people who need them can access them.

My research has been supported by grants from the National Institute of Mental Health and non-profit organizations, and is sustained by numerous collaborations with researchers across institutions and fields.
