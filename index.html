About me

Hi! I am a PhD candidate in clinical psychology at Drexel University. I obtained a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology at Rutgers University and a Master of Science in Psychology at Drexel University.

As a researcher and clinician with experience working across academic and industry settings, my overall goal is to make evidence-based mental health care more accessible for those who need it. A major focus of my work is on leveraging digital technology to study mental health conditions in daily life and develop, test, and implement scalable mental health supports for specific populations and settings.

I deeply value collaboration and mentorship, and I welcome opportunities to work with others who have similar interests. Please reach out if you'd like to connect!

Overall goals of my work

  • Making mental health care more accessible
  • Improving translation of mental health research to practice
  • Doing science that meets the needs and priorities of the communities it aims to serve

Methods I prioritize

  • Technology-enabled mental health solutions
  • Co-developing programs with end-users
  • Dissemination and implementation science
  • Cross-field and sector collaborations
  • Mixed methods research
