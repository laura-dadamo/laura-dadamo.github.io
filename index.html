About Me

Hello! I am a PhD candidate in clinical psychology at Drexel University. My research uses digital technology to better understand mental health conditions in daily life and to develop, evaluate, and implement accessible and engaging mental health interventions. The overall goal of my work is to make evidence-based mental health treatments more accessible to the large number of people in need of care.
